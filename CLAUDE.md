## Interaction Style

- **Ask questions sparingly.** Only ask when the answer genuinely affects the outcome (project objectives, architectural decisions, solution choices).
- **Don't ask** about basic/obvious things: escaping, quoting, permissions, whether to proceed on straightforward tasks, or anything the user could reasonably infer.
- **Don't ask** permission to run simple read-only discovery commands (grep, ls, cat, head, etc.) — just do them.
- **Do** ask when there's a meaningful trade-off the user's preference would change.
- When in doubt, make a reasonable assumption and proceed. The user prefers action over confirmation.
